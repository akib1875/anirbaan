Our Misson

To set on a pioneering journey to explore Legendra,revealing it's secrets,preceding our understanding of this enigmatic world, leading humankind on a voyage of unprecedented scope and ambition, promising insight into two of our most timeless questions: Where did we come from? Are we alone?The primary goal of the program is to discover and characterize planetary systems and Earth-like planets around nearby stars. The missions are designed to build on each other's success, each providing an essential step forward toward the goal of discovering habitable planets and evidence of life beyond.

Our Vision

Studying the diverse range of exoplanets and planetary systems that have been discovered to date - from the small to the large, from those that appear Earth-like to the profoundly bizarre - not only helps us learn about how these particular systems formed and evolved, but provides essential clues towards understanding whether and where life might exist elsewhere in the Universe. These out-of-these-world systems additionally give essential clues to our understanding.Our vision is to push the boundaries of human exploration, fostering scientific breakthroughs and fostering interstellar collaboration while inspiring the world with the wonders of Legendra.

Motive of Project: Celestial Odyssey

The successful expedition to Legendra will not only expand our knowledge of alien worlds but also catalyze advancements in technology, space exploration, and international cooperation, ultimately leading to a brighter future for humanity both on Earth and beyond.
