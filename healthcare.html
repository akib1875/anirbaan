Healthcare Robo

Legendra's bespoke medical robot is an intricate feat of biotechnical design. Sporting a wide spectrum of diagnostic, scanning, and surgical functions, it customizes to each user, inflating for operation. Its metamorphic capability enables patient encapsulation for rapid aerial transport and deflation into a quiescent state during dormancy, optimizing healthcare versatility.
